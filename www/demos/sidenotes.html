Sidenotes

Sidenotes are created with the <small role=note> element. Sidenotes are supplemental notes or comments placed in the margins of a page, typically alongside the main text. They provide additional information, context, or commentary related to the adjacent text.

Sidenotes must be visually and semantically distinct even when the viewport isn't wide enough to facilitate their normal placement. This page has a few sidenotes which are implemented using markup similar to the example below.
